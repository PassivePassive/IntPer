About

Interior Perspective Limited is a growing and uniquely positioned design and fit-out firm that merges interior design with branding. With our foresight and holistic perspective, we cross disciplines and boundaries to bring innovation to design projects including cultural, residential, retail and creative workplaces.



Job Description

We are seeking a highly communicative, organised, motivated, self-directed and talented creative business development intern to join our dynamic creative team in Nairobi for a 3 month internship program that may lead to a fulltime position based on performance.

  1. Work with Design Principals to develop a strategic business development plan for each market sector, determine target clients and execute the strategy.
  2. Assess and develop new markets, trends, and business opportunities to establish new client relationships.
  3. Research potential clients and institutions in each market sector to determine
  4. Establish and maintain a business development database

  3. Demonstrate use of technology to drive the business.

  5. Excellent organizational, people, verbal and written communication skills.
  6. Top-notch design and execution skills of client’s presentation.
