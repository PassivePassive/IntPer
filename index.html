Welcome to Interior Perspective

design & Build

Interior Perspective Ltd is an Interior Design studio defining spaces with vision, visual interest while preserving function. We specialize in Residential Interiors, Commercial Interiors and Product Design. By immersing ourselves in each client’s culture, we create spaces that tell a meaningful story about the perspective of those who bring them to life.

Meet Our Team

Clara’s background in technology created a stepping stone to actualise her passion in design. She studied BSc. IT at JKUAT. Worked in the industry as a Network Administrator before pursuing her post graduate diploma in Interior Design at Buruburu Institute of Fine Arts in Nairobi.

She has a strong background in space planning, computer-aided design and project management from her experience working with two top Interior firms in the country. She is result oriented and a creative communicator with an eye for detail.
