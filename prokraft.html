Project Info

Client:

Prokraft Africa

Location:

Blue Violet Plaza, Kindaruma Road

Brief:

Design Proposal and Fit Out

Area:

133m2

Completed:

2015

ProKraft Africa is a premier content , art and advertising production house that works with the best talent available in the continent.

For this project, we co-designed with one of their in-house photographers, Mutua Matheka. We came up with the concepts and general look and feel of the space. The studio had an open layout, basic white walls and a dark slab. To make the space warm and inviting, we used reclaimed wood for column cladding and industrial pipes for lighting.
